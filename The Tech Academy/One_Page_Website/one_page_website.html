About

  The "Banksy Bank" is a collection of pictures of street art projects done by the artist who goes by the name Banksy. The navigation bar above can be used to go to the photo gallery of Banksy's pieces and to the contact form so you can reach out to us with information about possible Banksy pieces you may encounter so we can add them to our bank. This single page website is a portfolio piece designed to show off my knowledge and usage of HTML and CSS.
